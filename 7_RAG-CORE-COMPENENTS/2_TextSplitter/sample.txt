Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. 
It allows computers to perform tasks that normally require human reasoning, 
such as decision-making, speech recognition, and visual perception.

Machine Learning is a subset of Artificial Intelligence. 
It focuses on training algorithms using data so that systems can improve automatically. 
Supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning are major categories.

Deep Learning is a specialized area of Machine Learning. 
It uses neural networks with multiple layers to process complex patterns. 
Deep learning powers technologies like image recognition and voice assistants.

Cricket is one of the most popular sports in the world.

The Indian Premier League is one of the biggest cricket tournaments globally.

Football is another global sport with billions of fans. 
Major tournaments like the FIFA World Cup attract international attention. 
Players like Messi and Ronaldo are known worldwide.

Healthy eating is essential for maintaining physical and mental well-being.
